The Price is Right Collector!








You will be given a random "retail price" at the start of the game.

There are four products below, each with their own hidden retail price. By clicking on a product you will add a specific amount of dollars to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the actual retail price listed above. You lose the game if your total score goes above the retail price.

The value of each product is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time a new game of The Price is Right Collector starts, the values on the products change too! Come on Down!
